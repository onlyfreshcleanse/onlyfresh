🥤 Know Your Detox
What is Cold-Pressed Juice?
🥤
Cold pressing uses hydraulic pressure instead of heat — preserving up to 3x more vitamins, enzymes and minerals than regular juicing. Every juice at Only Fresh is made this way.
3x more nutrients No heat damage Live enzymes Zero sugar added
Why Detox Your Body?
🌿
Your liver, kidneys and skin constantly filter toxins from food, air and stress. Fresh detox juices with amla, ginger and greens support this natural process — giving you more energy, clearer skin and better digestion.
Flushes toxins Boosts energy Clears skin Aids digestion
Best Time to Drink Detox Juice
⏰
Morning on empty stomach is ideal — your body absorbs nutrients fastest. Green Detox first thing in the morning kickstarts metabolism and hydrates cells after overnight fasting.
Morning empty stomach 30 mins before meals Post workout
